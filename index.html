About Us

This workshop is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the security aspects related to Central Processing Units (CPUs) and Graphics Processing Units (GPUs), two critical components in modern computing systems. In this workshop, we will delve into the intricacies of CPU and GPU architectures, their operational principles, and the security challenges they face in today‚Äôs digital landscape. We will explore how these components can become potential targets for malicious activities and what measures can be taken to mitigate such threats.

What is Our Goal?

This workshop is intended for students(UGs and PGs) who are interested in computer hardware, Computer Architecture Security or both. Whether you are a seasoned professional or a curious beginner, this workshop will equip you with the knowledge and skills to better understand and address CPU-GPU security issues. Our sessions will cover a range of topics including but not limited to:

  • Understanding the architecture of CPUs and GPUs.
  • Exploring the vulnerabilities inherent in these components.
  • Discussing the latest security threats and breaches involving CPUs and GPUs.
  • Learning about preventive measures and best practices for securing these critical components.
  • Hands-on sessions on security tools and techniques for CPUs and GPUs.

We look forward to your active participation and engagement in this journey towards a more secure computing environment!
